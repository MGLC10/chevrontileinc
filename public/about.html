About ChevronTileInc.

ChevronTileInc. is a San Diego-based tile installation company dedicated to delivering high-quality residential and commercial tile solutions. With over 40+ years of hands-on experience, we proudly serve homeowners and businesses across San Diego County with precision, reliability, and attention to detail.

From coastal homes in La Jolla to modern spaces in downtown San Diego, we bring expert craftsmanship to every project. We specialize in kitchen backsplashes, bathroom remodels, flooring, and custom tile designs using materials built to handle Southern California living.

We believe every project is a partnership. That is why we work closely with each client to understand their vision, recommend the right materials, and deliver results that match both style and function. Our goal is to make the process smooth, professional, and stress-free from start to finish.

Whether you are updating a single room or completing a larger residential or commercial project, ChevronTileInc. is committed to quality craftsmanship and lasting results. We take pride in helping San Diego property owners create spaces that are both beautiful and built to last.
