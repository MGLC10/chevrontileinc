Our Services

ChevronTileInc. provides professional tile installation services throughout San Diego County. We focus on high-quality workmanship, clean finishes, and reliable service for both residential and commercial projects.

  • Kitchen Backsplashes – Clean, modern designs that enhance style and protect your walls.
  • Bathroom Remodels – Tile installation for floors, showers, and full bathroom upgrades.
  • Shower Surrounds – Waterproof, durable, and custom tile shower designs.
  • Flooring Installation – Tile flooring for living spaces, kitchens, and commercial properties.
  • Commercial Tile Work – Reliable installation for offices, retail, and business spaces.
  • Custom Tile Designs – Unique layouts including chevron, herringbone, and mosaic styles.
  • Tile Repair & Restoration – Fixing damaged tiles, grout work, and refinishing.
  • Outdoor & Pool Areas – Durable tile solutions for patios and poolside environments.

Every project is completed with attention to detail and a commitment to long-lasting results. Whether you're upgrading your home or improving a commercial space, Chevron Tile Inc. delivers quality you can trust.
